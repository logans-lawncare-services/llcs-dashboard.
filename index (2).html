Welcome to LLCS dashboard
Keep track of jobs, expenses, and money for Logan’s Lawncare Services in one place.

Use the navigation to move between sections:

  • Expenses: Submit and approve expenses.
  • Jobs: Add work, payments, and notes.
  • Jobs columned: Kanban view by status.
  • Charts: See patterns in your work and money.
  • Admin: Manage accounts, totals, and exports.

All data is stored locally in this browser for your LLCS GitHub Pages site.
